Chicken Gyros

Prep: 15 min Marinate: 30 min Cook: 10 min Serves: 4
Servings: 4
⬇ Download PDF

"A vibrant Greek classic. The secret tool here is full-fat Greek yogurt, which beautifully tenderizes lean chicken tenderloins while locking in bold aromatics, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, and earthy spices."

Ingredients

Marinade & Chicken

  • 1½ lbs chicken tenderloins (or breast/thighs)
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt, whole milk preferred
  • 1 large lemon, juiced
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil (+ 1 tbsp for skillet)
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp dried Greek oregano
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Kosher salt and black pepper to taste

For Serving

  • 4–6 Greek pita breads
  • 1 cup tzatziki sauce
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, cored and sliced
  • 1 small red onion, halved and sliced
  • Pitted Kalamata olives

Instructions

  1. Whisk the marinade: In a large mixing bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, lemon juice, 2 tbsp olive oil, and red wine vinegar. Whisk in the minced garlic, oregano, paprika, cumin, coriander, salt, pepper, and optional cayenne until fully emulsified.
  2. Marinate the chicken: Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and toss until each piece is uniformly coated. Cover tightly and refrigerate for 30 minutes, or up to overnight.
  3. Sear the chicken: Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a heavy non-stick or cast-iron pan over medium-high heat. Pull the chicken from the bowl, shaking off any loose drippings. Arrange in a single flat layer. Cook undisturbed for 5 minutes until heavily browned, then flip and cook another 5 minutes until cooked through to 165°F internal temperature.
  4. Warm the pitas: Warm pita flatbreads over an open medium gas burner flame or in a hot, dry skillet until warm and pliable.
  5. Assemble: Transfer a warm pita to a serving plate. Slather a thick dollop of tzatziki down the spine, then top with 2–3 strips of seared chicken, fresh tomato, cucumber, green pepper, red onion, and Kalamata olives. Fold tightly and enjoy.

Alternate Cooking Methods

  • Grill: Fire up the grill to medium heat. Cook tenders 5 minutes per side until done.
  • Oven: Heat oven to 375°F. Bake on a parchment-lined sheet pan for approximately 20 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165°F.
